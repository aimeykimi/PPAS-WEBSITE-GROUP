The Info Counter at Perbadanan Perpustakaan Awam Selangor serves as a one-stop center for assistance, guidance, and inquiries. Our friendly staff is ready to provide you with accurate and reliable information.

Info Counter Services:

Please visit the Info Counter for assistance or contact us at info@ppas.gov.my


Available Services

-General inquiries about library services and facilities

-Guidance on library membership and account setup

-Assistance with locating books and resources

-Details on events, workshops, and library programs

-Lost and found services
