Welcome to the Makerspace at Perbadanan Perpustakaan Awam Selangor! Our Makerspace is a creative hub where visitors of all ages can explore, innovate, and learn new skills.

What We Offer:

  • 3D Printing Services: Turn your digital designs into physical objects.
  • Laser Cutting and Engraving: Create precise designs on wood, acrylic, and more.
  • Electronics and Robotics: Explore circuits, programming, and robotics projects.
  • Workshops and Training: Join our hands-on classes on various tech and creative topics.
  • Collaboration Spaces: Work with like-minded individuals on exciting projects.

How to Get Started:

Our Makerspace is open to all library members. To access the equipment or attend a workshop, please visit the info counter at PPAS or contact us for more details.

For inquiries, please call Encik Amin (+603-1234 5678) or email us at makerspace@ppas.gov.my.
