READING SECTION FACILITIES

Back

The Reading Section at Perbadanan Perpustakaan Awam Selangor provides a quiet, comfortable space for all library visitors to enjoy reading and studying. Whether you're looking for a peaceful environment or a place to focus on your work, our reading facilities cater to all your needs.

Reading Section Facilities:

Our Reading Section is equipped with various amenities to ensure a pleasant and productive visit. For more information, please contact us at info@ppas.gov.my.


Available Facilities:
  • - Comfortable seating areas for individual reading
  • - Group study spaces for collaborative learning
  • - Access to a wide range of books, journals, and e-resources
  • - Computer stations with internet access for research purposes
  • - Silent zones to promote a quiet reading environment
  • - Air-conditioned spaces for comfort during your visit


Operating Hours:

Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Saturday - Sunday: 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM
